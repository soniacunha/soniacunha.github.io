Neighbourhood

This project took part of an international summer school - Big Reset on Neighborhood Design.

In a city with the most valuable agricultural land in Portugal and next to Porto, the aim of the concept was to take advantage of these conditions.  Thus, in Castêlo da Maia, a small city on an increasing expansion and without its own symbol, the focus was to create an agricultural network. We propose to dedicate the vast fields to the agricultural production with the expectation that in a few years they can expand. Its privileged location, near the tram, makes it possible to quickly move the produce between, and within, Maia and Porto.

The concept evolved to 7 themes: production, community field, backyard, green education, food factory, metro market and slow food train. An old building was restored as the center of all this process - Food Market - where citizens can sell their products, gather and share ideas. A bicycle loop connects the metro and the agricultural fields and the schools.
