Neighbourhood

This project took part of an international summer school - Big Reset on Neighbourhood Design.

The objective was to take advantage of the conditions offered by Portugal's most valuable agricultural land. Thus, in Castêlo da Maia, an expanding parish without its own symbol, the focus was to create an agricultural network that would become the hallmark of this place.

The concept evolved to 7 themes: production, community field, backyard, green education, food factory, metro market and slow food train. We propose to dedicate the vast fields to the agricultural production with the expectation that in a few years they can expand. Its privileged location, near the tram, makes it possible to quickly move the produce between, and within, Maia and Porto.

An old building was restored as the center of all this process - Food Market - where citizens can sell their products, gather and share ideas.

A bicycle loop connects the metro and the agricultural fields and the schools.
