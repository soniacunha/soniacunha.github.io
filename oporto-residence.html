Oporto Residence

This project is a housing complex located in one of the busiest areas of Porto next to Casa da Música. The project is integrated in an urban plan composed by four buildings between 4 and 7 levels, with offices, stores and apartments from (T1 to T4); all the buildings open towards the backyard.

The gallery building is oriented North-South. The entrances, kitchens and bathrooms are turned north, allowing the living room and the bedrooms to open with large windows to the interior of the block, which is located to the south.

The apartment module is simple, making a great distinction between the great fluidity of the common areas, and the well delimited private areas.

A wood volume, created by the guest’s toilet, defines and organizes the kitchen and the living room. This element also provides privacy between the entrance hall and the living room; so when someone opens the front door there is no visual contact with the this area. To see this volume as a loose piece, there are no walls attached to it and the swing doors next to it don’t have a frame around them.
