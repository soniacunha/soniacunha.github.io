Oporto Residence

This project is a housing complex located in one of the busiest areas of Porto next to Casa da Música. Composed by four buildings between 4 and 7 levels, with, offices, stores and apartments from (T1 to T4); all the buildings open towards the backyard.

The kitchen and the toilets face the gallery, which is oriented north. This gives the opportunity to open widely the living areas to the interior of the block. All of the rooms facing south have a balcony.

The apartment module is very simple. The private areas of the apartment (bedrooms) have well defined limits. The bedrooms are connected by a private corridor disconnected from the common areas. The kitchen and the living room have a different organization. A wood volume, created by the guest’s toilet, defines and organizes these spaces. This element also provides privacy between the entrance hall and the living room; so when someone opens the front door there is no visual contact with the living room. To see this volume as a loose piece, there are no walls attached to it and the swing doors next to it don’t have a frame around them.
