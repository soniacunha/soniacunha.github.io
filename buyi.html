The way of the Buyi

 

360º

This project was designed for the 120 Hours contest of 2016 in collaboration with Nuno da Graça Ribeiro. The program was a canteen and a library with the total of 100m2 to accommodate 200 children of the LiangMeng School complex.

The project was to be built in an underdeveloped part of China, which meant that these functions needed to be solved in a very rudimentary and pragmatic way.

 

Because of this, we looked to the vernacular constructions, in search for clues. The project is composed by two simple volumes connected by a small corridor. It’s built with the locally available stone and wood; however, it denotes its contemporaneity through its form and openness towards the central patio that can host different activities.

Because it was such a small space, both rooms can interchange its activities. The library space also has special tables that can fold to create an open space for all kinds of activities that the kids and teachers might want to perform.
