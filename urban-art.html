Center for Urban Art

A Center for Urban Art, on the heart of Porto city, built where there was a 20 meters high rock.

The set intends to recreate the sensations felt in the urban grid of the historical center, with its corners and mysterious spaces that are discovered while wandering the narrow streets. Thus, the 4 volumes made of concrete hide a square at the highest level that creates a place to appreciate the exposed objects of art. As the concert bar is connected to this gathering point, the square also offers the right place to relax while listening to a concert on the outside. The stairs path close to the train station, which leads to this square, also provides the appreciation of art on the outside, as the “natural space for urban art”.

 

Each volume has its own function such as auditorium, exposition room, educational rooms, multi-purpose room and main entrance, however those 4 volumes are connected on the ground floor.

This is a place where you can find art both on the inside and on the outside.
