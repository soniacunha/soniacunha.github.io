This project built on site was an ephemeral installation in collaboration with falaatelier as part of the Get Set Festival in May of 2015.

The object is a spatial experience located on a park in the center of Porto with the purpose of giving new sensations to the users and let them become more engaged with their environment. Is a labyrinth where each “room” has its own identity that the user is invited to discover. The openings are made with circular holes on the cardboard and each one has a different size so some of the rooms are inaccessible because the hole is to small to trespass it. This gives a mysterious atmosphere to the labyrinth, especially when the hole is too high to reach it, so you will always wonder what is inside the room.
